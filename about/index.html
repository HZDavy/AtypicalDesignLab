AtypicalDesignLab ©

非典型设计研究室是一个创意设计研究机构，成立于2021年，致力于本土化的当代独立设计方法研究与实践。

通过应用跨学科的方法和前沿设计理论，在本土设计学领域开展系统性研究。

-

Atypical Design Lab is a creative design research institution, established in 2021, dedicated to the research and practice of localized contemporary independent design methods.

By applying interdisciplinary approaches and cutting-edge design theories, it conducts systematic research in the field of local design studies.
